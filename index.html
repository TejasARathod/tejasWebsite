Projects

Facial Emotion Recognition Using Deep Siamese Neural Networks with Single-Emotion and Multi-Emotion Models from Kids to Adults.

This research introduces an innovative emotion recognition algorithm using Siamese networks (Inception Resnet V2 and VGG19), achieving groundbreaking results with SCNN-IRV2 (96-99%) and SCNN-VGG19 (95-99%) models. The project also pioneers a comprehensive spontaneous emotion dataset for teenagers, addressing a resource gap and advancing research.

Final Year Project - AgriDoc (Agriculture Doctor) - An Autonomous AI Agricultural Robot for Weed and Disease Detection

This Jetson Nano-powered agricultural robot project embraced ROS architecture for optimized LiDAR setup. It seamlessly integrated Mapping, Navigation, and diverse functionalities like Teleoperation, perception, and computer vision. Disease detection achieved a high average confidence level of 99.51% using a specialized CNN architecture. Weed detection, employing YOLOv5, secured a robust F1 score of 0.95. The project also featured an autonomous spraying mechanism for precise weedicide application in precision farming.

Multipurpose Deep Learning-Powered UAV for Forest Fire Prevention and Emergency Response

Introducing a customized UAV for Forest Rescue: Equipped with a robust quadcopter frame, Raspberry Pi camera for live streaming, GSM module for contactless communication, and Neo-6M GPS module for precise localization. The UAV features a motor-controlled first aid kit and employs deep learning for forest fire prediction with GANs, achieving 90.7% accuracy. Streamlined by Raspberry Pi4 and GUI, ensuring a rapid emergency response.

Robocon 2022

The theme of ROBOCON 2022 was centered around the concept of Lagori, models of robots to engage in a competitive game between two teams: Team 1, known as the "Seeker," and Team 2, referred to as the "Hitter." The game commenced with the seeker launching a ball to target a stone tower named "Lagori," aiming to break it. Following this, the seekers' objective was to reconstruct the stone tower by piling up the stones. Meanwhile, the hitter team had the task of throwing balls to disrupt the seeker team's efforts and impede their progress in rebuilding the tower.

Sight Beyond Sight

Our website transforms online content accessibility for the visually impaired using AI. By inputting a URL, our system summarizes text with OpenAI's model and describes images with Google Cloud Vision API. Redis caching ensures fast data access, and voice control allows effortless inquiries. Our innovation enables inclusive web experiences with natural speech interaction.
