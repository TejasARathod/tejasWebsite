About Me Greetings! I'm Tejas Rathod, currently serving as a Deep Learning Intern at AI4M, a pioneering Computer Vision startup in Pune. Here, I'm passionately bridging the gap between cutting-edge research and engineering solutions, leveraging the robust NVIDIA platform and AWS infrastructure to tackle industry-level challenges head-on. With a B.Tech in Electronics and Telecommunication from Symbiosis Institute of Technology, Pune, I'm now poised to elevate my expertise by pursuing a Masters in Computer Science.
